Hi, I'm Murat!

I was born in Ankara, Turkey on April 22nd, 1989. I obtained my B.Sc. degree in Mechatronics Engineering department from Kocaeli University, Turkey, in 2013. Having successfully completed bachelor's studies, I started my MS in the same department. I was then granted with a full academic scholarship for my graduate studies abroad by Ministry of National Education, Turkey.
My research areas are Signal Processing, Machine Vision, Computer Vision, Robotics.
